From Planning to Completion, We've Got You Covered

Pro App is Project management software that
allows you to organise projects,
track work efficiently, and
engage with your team from anywhere.

Use Cases

Your one-stop solution for efficient project management

Our app is designed to help you manage your projects and tasks seamlessly. With our user-friendly
interface, you can easily create and track your projects, assign tasks to team members, and monitor
progress.

Projects

* Create new projects, assign team members, and set deadlines
* View all projects, sorted by status (yet to start, in progress, completed)
* Get an overview of all your projects on the dashboard

Tasks

* Create new tasks, assign team members, and set deadlines
* View all tasks in one place assigned to multiple users
* Get notifications for task updates

Chat

* Communicate with team members using our integrated chat feature
* Collabrate with ease with your teammates


Settings

* Manage your profile, change your password, and logout

Popup-Notification

* Get real-time notifications for new tasks, updates, and messages
